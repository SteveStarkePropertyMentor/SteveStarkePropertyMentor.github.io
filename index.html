About

I've coached with Sean Wood, Grant Hoey, Steve Goodey, Pion Consulting, Wealth Ninja, and other leading NZ Property Brands

With impressive negotiation, coaching, and direct property investment experience, my experience spans financial planning, real estate, personal property acquisition and sales, substantial renovation projects, and more. An astute investor on a personal level, I am equally as comfortable in the commercial sector.

I have also built a strong reputation as an industry mentor, helping others achieve property stability and success, helping budding investors grow their wealth through property to create a secure future for themselves and their families. I live the importance of building foundations for the next generation.

My down-to-earth and honest approach resonates with my clients. Having worked the hard yards to get where I am today, I have also added academic study to my knowledge base, having achieved a Post Graduate Diploma in Business Administration, and National Certificates in both Financial Advice and Real Estate.

No longer a Financial Adviser, I am focussed on the most tangible substantial asset you will commonly see and deal with in your life – property. I have been a real estate licensee salesperson since 2014, during which time I have been Project Marketing Manager, Sales Manager, and Business Growth Manager (all the same time). Latterly at REMAX New Zealand, part of the largest real estate brand in the world. In 2020, I was proud to be a key player in a team that achieved #1 for REMAX Global, Owner Teams (Don Ha). That is no small achievement. I won Platinum Sales status during that time, have run my own real estate franchise, and have also been Certified as a Guild Member of the Institute for Luxury Home Marketing.

I've extensive coaching experience in class and field spanning some 16+ years across multiple property types. A former General Manager and Tutor at PropertyTutors, Director at other leading companies, and former Lead Coach for Steve Goodey Property Coaching, I have the experience, the networks, the chops and the tools, to help you get to where you need to be.
